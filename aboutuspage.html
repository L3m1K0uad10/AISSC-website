Who We Are

Africa International Students Service Center (AISSC) S.A.R.L. is a leading intermediary dedicated to connecting African students with top-tier educational institutions abroad. Our mission is to empower students to achieve their academic and career aspirations by providing comprehensive support throughout their journey, from initial consultation to post-arrival assistance. We are proud partners with renowned universities like KIIT University, ensuring access to quality education and global opportunities.

Our Services

Initial Consultation

Personalized guidance to understand your academic goals and preferences.

Application & Admission Support

Expert assistance with university applications, ensuring all requirements are met.

Visa Application & Pre-Departure Guidance

Comprehensive support for visa applications and pre-departure preparations.

Post-Arrival Support

Ongoing support to help you settle in and succeed in your new academic environment.
